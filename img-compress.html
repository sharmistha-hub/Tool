Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to image size? +

We currently support images up to 10MB in size. For larger images, consider resizing before uploading or using our resize options during compression.

Are my images stored anywhere? +

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What's the best compression level? +

For most purposes, we recommend starting with 80% quality. This typically reduces file size significantly while maintaining excellent visual quality. Adjust higher for critical images or lower for maximum compression.

Which format should I choose? +

WebP generally offers the best compression. JPEG is best for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency. Our tool will recommend the optimal format based on your image content.
